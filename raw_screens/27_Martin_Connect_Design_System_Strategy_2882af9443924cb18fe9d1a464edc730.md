# Martin Connect Design System Blueprint

## Brand Identity
- **Product:** Martin Connect
- **Category:** Premium Hiring & Recruitment Platform (B2B/B2C)
- **Personality:** Professional, trustworthy, technology-driven, premium, global, scalable
- **Visual Style:** Clean enterprise SaaS — sharp edges, strong hierarchy, dense-but-organised.

## Color Tokens
- **Primary Blue:** `#1E63F3` (Core brand color)
- **Dark Blue:** `#0F3FBF` (Headings & accents)
- **Light Blue:** `#2DA8FF` (Interactive elements & accents)
- **Navy Black:** `#081126` (Sidebars & deep backgrounds)
- **Background White:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Surface Off-White:** `#F7F9FC`
- **Border Light:** `#E8EEF7`
- **Text Muted:** `#8A9BB8`
- **Success Green:** `#16A34A`
- **Warning Amber:** `#D97706`
- **Danger Red:** `#DC2626`
- **Gradient:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #0F3FBF, #2DA8FF)`

## Typography
- **Headings:** DM Sans Bold (Primary) / Manrope Bold (Secondary)
- **Body:** DM Sans Regular
- **Data/Code:** IBM Plex Mono
- **Scale:** 12, 14, 16, 18, 20, 24, 28, 32, 40, 48, 56px

## Corner Radii
- **Small:** 4px (Buttons, badges)
- **Medium:** 8px (Form inputs, sidebar items)
- **Large/Card:** 12px (Job cards, modals, containers)
- **Pill:** 20px (Status pills, filter chips)

## Grid System
- **Desktop:** 12-column, 24px gutter, 1280px max-width.
- **Tablet:** 768px.
- **Mobile:** 375px.

## Core Component Patterns
- **Navigation:** Dark navbar (#081126), mega-menus, icon-based sidebars.
- **Search:** Hero search (56px height, rounded-12), inline search.
- **Cards:** White bg, 12px radius, shadow-sm, hover state with blue left border.
- **Forms:** Labels above inputs, IBM Plex Mono for numeric/salary data.
- **Status Pills:** Color-coded (Applied: Blue, Shortlisted: Green, etc.)
- **Overlays:** Blur backdrops, right-side slide drawers for details.
