Popular Questions

How do I optimize my profile for industrial roles? expand_more
Ensure your technical certifications and project experience are documented in the Skills section. Our algorithm prioritizes verified credentials in industrial engineering and supply chain management.
What are the benefits of the Recruiter Premium tier? expand_more
Premium users gain access to AI-powered candidate ranking, unlimited direct messaging, and advanced analytics dashboards for tracking hiring velocity.
How can I track my application status? expand_more
Navigate to 'My Applications' in your dashboard. Status pills indicate whether your profile is 'Under Review', 'Shortlisted', or 'Hired'.
