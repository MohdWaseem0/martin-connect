Privacy Policy

At Martin Connect, we take your privacy seriously. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information within our recruitment ecosystem.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all services provided by Martin Connect. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Our goal is to connect top-tier talent with industry-leading companies while maintaining the highest standards of data integrity.

2. Data Collection

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you:

  • check_circle Personal Identity: Full name, email address, phone number, and professional social profiles.
  • check_circle Professional Data: Resumes, cover letters, employment history, education, and skill certifications.
  • check_circle Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, and navigation patterns on the Martin Connect platform.

3. Data Usage

Candidate Matching

Using proprietary algorithms to connect your professional profile with relevant job openings that match your expertise.

Service Optimization

Monitoring site performance and user interaction to refine the hiring pipeline and dashboard experience.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other international privacy frameworks, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

Right Description
Access Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
Erasure The "Right to be Forgotten" - request permanent deletion of your profile.
Correction Update inaccurate or incomplete professional information.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Essential

We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Cookie Consent

6. Security Measures

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We employ enterprise-grade encryption and regular security audits to mitigate risks.
