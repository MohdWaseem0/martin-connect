About Razorpay

Razorpay is India's leading full-stack financial solutions company, helping Indian businesses with comprehensive and innovative solutions built over robust technology to address the entire length and breadth of the payment and banking journey.

Established in 2014, the company provides technology to accept, process, and disburse payments with its product suite. It gives access to all payment modes including credit card, debit card, net banking, UPI, and popular wallets including JioMoney, Mobikwik, Airtel Money, FreeCharge, Ola Money, and PayZapp.
